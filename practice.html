The Panda Bear

About Pandas

The Panda Bear, also known as, great pandas,
parti-colored bears, bamboo bears and white bears,
are the treasured bear of china. Being an endangered
species, there are only about 1,900 left around the
world. 300 of which are in zoos.

Species

  • Giant Panda
  • Red Panda

Features

Giant pandas grow to be 27 to 32 inches
(70 - 80 centimeters) tall at the shoulder, 4 to 5 feet
(1.2 to 1.5 meters) long and can weigh up
to 275 lbs. While the Pandas distant relative, the Red Panda, is only 20 to 26 inches (50 to 65 cm) tall and weighs 10 to 20 lbs.

Habitat

Regions of Origin:

  1. Mountainous regions of China
  2. Sichuan
  3. Shaanxi and Gansu provinces
